Let's Follow Some Rules in Our Friendship

  • No Devices After 10.30PM (Strict): This rule is for ensuring you get proper sleep and maintain better health.
  • 30 Min Daily Talk on Snapchat: Let's spend quality time together by being with just each other, alone and chatting every day.
  • We'll Prioritize Friendship Over Problems: We'll handle our issues calmly, remembering that our fight is against the problem, not each other.
  • We'll Irritate Each Other Within Limits: Just like best friends do, we'll tease each other, but always in moderation!
  • We'll Always Be Honest: Let's help each other improve by sharing constructive feedback and striving for success.
  • Never Be Ignorant or Silent: Just like rule no. 3, if something bothers us, we'll talk it out. Fighting saves bonds; silence doesn't.
  • BFF Forever: Our friendship is everlasting! Our love and bond will go on forever.
